Common questions

Does Salah Logbook track me?

No. Salah Logbook does not use advertising trackers, cross-app tracking, or personal analytics. Your data stays on your device.

Where is my prayer data stored?

Your prayer log is stored locally on your device. It is not uploaded to any server.

Why are prayer times different from my mosque?

Prayer times are calculated estimates and may differ depending on calculation method, Asr school, location, and local mosque adjustments. Please follow your local mosque timetable where required.

Can I change my city?

Yes. You can change your selected city in the Settings tab at any time. The app will re-download prayer times for the new city.

Can I turn off reminders?

Yes. Reminders are optional and can be managed in the app's Settings tab or in iOS Settings under Notifications.

Which calculation method should I choose?

This depends on your location and community practice. Common choices include Muslim World League (global), ISNA (North America), Umm al-Qura (Saudi Arabia), and others. Follow your local mosque or trusted Islamic authority where uncertain.

How do I export my prayer log?

You can export your full prayer history as a CSV file from the Settings tab using the "Export as CSV" option.
