🛡️ Privacy Policy

We don't track you.

Salah Logbook is designed to be private and simple. Your prayer log stays on your device.

Effective date: June 7, 2026

Information We Do Not Collect

Salah Logbook does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information.

We do not use:

  • Advertising trackers
  • Third-party analytics trackers
  • User profiling
  • Cross-app tracking
  • Data brokers

Prayer Log Data

Prayer log data, including prayed and missed prayer records, is stored locally on your device.

This may include:

  • Prayer status
  • Prayer date
  • Prayer name
  • App settings such as reminders, selected city, calculation method, and display preferences

This information is not sent to us.

City and Prayer Time Data

If you select a city, the app may use the city name, latitude, longitude, timezone, calculation method, and Asr school to download prayer times from a prayer time provider.

This is used only to calculate or download prayer times for your selected location.

The app does not use this information for advertising or tracking.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app uses local iOS notifications to remind you before prayer times.

Notification settings are handled by iOS and can be changed in your device settings at any time.

Internet Access

The app may connect to a prayer time API to download prayer times for your selected city.

The app does not use this connection for advertising or tracking.

Data Storage

Your prayer log is stored locally on your device.

Deleting the app may delete locally stored app data unless iOS or iCloud backups preserve it.

Children's Privacy

Salah Logbook does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
